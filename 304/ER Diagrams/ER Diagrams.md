ER diagrams are a way to represent Relational [[Database|Databases]].

- Rectangles represent entities
- Diamonds represent Relations
- Ovals represent attributes
- lines represent relationships between entities and relationships, or entities and attributes
- [[Weak Entity| Weak entities]] are represented with thick borderlines 
- Relations can be 1-1, 1-many, or many-many
	- Differences are shown with regular lines, or arrowed lines
- Entities cannot have lines connecting them, they must be shown with relationships
- Entities can participate in an [[IsA]], shown with a triangle, and participation can be [[Total]], [[Partial]], [[Disjoint]] or [[Overlapping]]
